# Commercial Execution Infrastructure — Category Positioning

**Canonical category definition:** `docs/CATEGORY_AND_POSITIONING.md`

This system is **not** an AI assistant, automation tool, bot, ChatGPT, or workflow builder.

It is:

- **Commercial execution infrastructure** — The universal, compliance-governed execution engine for all commercial communication across every industry.
- **Governed communication** — All customer text is template-based with structured variables. AI output is structured JSON only.
- **Compliance-first execution** — Compliance enforced pre-send. Disclosures, consent, and jurisdiction rules applied deterministically.
- **Structured strategy engine** — Full lifecycle states, objection trees, and escalation rules. No AI invents states or branches probabilistically.
- **Cross-party confirmation system** — Recorded commitments, disclosure acknowledgements, and proof capsules. Append-only.
- **Deterministic execution pipeline** — Every action emitted as action_intents. No freeform AI text. No probabilistic branching.

## What it does

- Handles **inbound** and **outbound** commercial communication
- Executes **voice calls** via intents and script blocks
- Qualifies leads using **structured strategy** and **objection trees**
- Enforces **compliance** (TCPA, fair housing, insurance, FDCPA, HIPAA-safe, etc.)
- Persists **structured memory** (lead_memory, emotional signals, strategy state)
- Governs **disclosures** and **consent**
- Escalates **intelligently** via deterministic channel and authority rules
- Records **commitments** and handles **financial obligations**
- Manages **multi-channel strategy** and **cross-party confirmation**

## What it does not do

- Generate unsafe freeform text to customers
- Violate compliance or branch probabilistically
- Expose internal state or IDs to customers
- Operate as a chatbot or generic AI assistant

## Solo appeal

- **Autopilot strict mode** — Single domain pack, editable templates, preview sandbox, approval toggle.
- **Simple channel escalation** — Rules-based; no AI decides.
- **Prebuilt industry preset** — Choose industry; live in 60 seconds with minimal configuration.

## Enterprise

- **Contract override** — enterprise_features_json for caps and overrides.
- **Jurisdiction lock** — approval_mode jurisdiction_locked.
- **Dual approval** — dual_approval_required.
- **Immutable archive** — Append-only message archive for regulator audit.
- **SLA** — Contract-driven; no fixed price.

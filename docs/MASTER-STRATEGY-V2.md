# Recall Touch — Master Strategy V2

Single source of truth for category, positioning, ICP, roadmap, and what not to build.

---

## Executive Verdict

- **What Recall Touch is:** The Revenue Execution OS — AI-powered system that ensures no opportunity dies from poor follow-up, missed communication, or human delay. Execution layer between existing tools and revenue: every inbound lead answered, every follow-up on time, every no-show recovered, every dead lead resurrected, every quote chased.
- **What it is NOT:** Generic AI receptionist, full CRM replacement, marketing automation, or cheap answering service. Never compete on "cheapest AI calls."
- **Where it wins:** Between "CRM that logs but doesn't act" and "human staff at $4K/mo that still misses calls." Local service businesses (dental, HVAC, legal, med spa, roofing, real estate) lose $5K–$50K/mo to missed calls and forgotten follow-ups.
- **Path to 9 figures:** Vertical domination of local service ($297–$2,400/mo ARPU) → multi-location/franchise ($5K–$25K/mo) → white-label to agencies (thousands of sub-accounts). Wedge = "answer every call, recover every missed opportunity."
- **Best immediate wedge:** Single-location home services and healthcare (dental, med spa, HVAC, plumbing, legal intake). Urgent pain, budget, fast onboarding, clear ROI.
- **Highest upside wedge:** Multi-location/franchise and agency/reseller channel.
- **Standalone vs acquisition:** Build for standalone scaling; acquisition optionality. Target $20–50M ARR for 10–15x strategic acquisition ($200M–$750M+).

## Product Vision

Recall Touch answers every missed call, qualifies every lead, books appointments, sends reminders, reduces no-shows, follows up on cold leads, chases quotes, reactivates past clients, and reports revenue recovered. Voice is hero; works across voice, SMS, email, web chat. Feels like a tireless, trained employee at <1 day receptionist salary. NOT a CRM, NOT marketing, NOT chatbot, NOT phone system — the execution layer.

---

## What to Cut / Not Build

- **Do not build:** Full CRM, email marketing platform, social media management, website builder, generic website chatbot, video/telehealth, inventory/POS, full accounting (invoice chasing yes, creation no), solo as primary target, free tier, AI image/doc/general assistant, WhatsApp (early), native mobile app (early), marketplace.
- **Do not dilute:** "AI writing assistant," "project management," "team chat," "document storage."
- **Expensive traps:** Real-time live transcription during call, custom voice cloning, multi-language beyond EN+ES in year 1.

## Roadmap Summary

- **30-day:** Core AI phone, provisioning, agent config, recording/transcript, test call, onboarding <10 min, revenue dashboard, SMS follow-up, calendar sync, 3 industry templates, voice polish, mobile dashboard.
- **90-day:** Follow-up sequences, no-show recovery, outbound, CRM (HubSpot + webhooks), agency sub-accounts, appointment reminders, quote follow-up, 3 more industry templates, usage/billing metering, referral, case studies, partner program.
- **12-month:** Visual workflow builder, reactivation engine, multi-location, white-label, campaign A/B, advanced analytics, lead scoring, review automation, compliance (HIPAA, consent), API, 10+ templates, ServiceTitan/Dentrix/Clio, team permissions, SSO.
- **24-month:** Custom AI pipeline, predictive analytics, benchmark intelligence, multi-language, international, enterprise compliance, AI coaching, payment collection in-call, native app, marketplace.

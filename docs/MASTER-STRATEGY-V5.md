# Recall Touch — Master Strategy V5 (Concise)

**Status:** ACTIVE — Source of truth  
**Date:** 2026-03-17  

## Category
- **Positioning:** **Revenue Execution OS** (not CRM, not generic assistant, not “cheap AI receptionist”)
- **Promise:** Every call answered. Every follow-up executed. Every opportunity captured.

## ICP focus (order)
1. **Single-location service businesses** (HVAC, dental, legal intake, med spa): fastest ROI, highest urgency
2. **Agency/reseller channel:** distribution leverage
3. **Multi-location/franchise:** highest ARPU + stickiness
4. **Solo/prosumer:** acquisition layer, but avoid leading revenue focus here

## What we win on
- **Execution, not organization:** system does the work (answer, qualify, book, follow up, recover, reactivate)
- **Vertical intelligence:** industry packs drive faster time-to-value
- **ROI attribution:** “revenue recovered” is the retention anchor

## “Do now or die” product priorities
- **Homepage rewrite + proof:** revenue-first positioning, trust, outcomes
- **Sub-3-minute onboarding:** industry → configured agent → test call
- **Revenue recovered as hero metric:** dominates dashboard hierarchy
- **Social proof:** testimonials, case studies, trust bar

## Voice strategy (margin + moat)
- Migrate from vendor voice to **self-hosted** stack over phases:
  - **STT:** Faster-Whisper (with VAD + endpointing)
  - **TTS:** Fish Speech (streaming; voice library; later cloning)
- **Targets:** <800ms turn latency, MOS >4.0, vendor fallback on failure

## Packaging philosophy
- Price on outcomes (revenue recovered), not raw features.
- Calls/numbers must remain profitable at scale; protect margins with self-hosted voice + carrier optimization.

## Homepage spec (V5)
- New hero copy + strong trust bar
- Outcome-first sections (problem → solution → how it works)
- **Mode selector** (Solo / Sales / Business)
- ROI calculator + industries + case studies + FAQ + final CTA

# Installation and Adoption Layer

This document describes the installation gating, snapshot, incident statements, and previews that make businesses perceive value within 24 hours while preserving doctrine (no dashboards, no analytics, no configuration UI).

## Phases

- **observing**: First 48 hours after messaging or payments connect. No automated outbound messages, no recovery actions. Only detection: stalled commitments, unresponded conversations, missed confirmations, overdue payments, and stalled opportunities are recorded in `observed_risk_events`. Imminent operational risk within 24h is recorded in `immediate_risk_events` and categories are tracked in `immediate_risk_categories_during_observing` for later counterfactual proof.
- **activation_ready**: After 48 hours. A loss snapshot can be generated (1–3 institutional sentences from observed risks). Automation remains off until the snapshot is seen and at least one unresolved immediate risk exists.
- **active**: Transition happens only when **snapshot_seen_at** is set **and** there is at least one unresolved `immediate_risk_event`. Activation is consequence of reality, not a manual choice. Automation engines are allowed; outbound recovery and morning certainty run.

## Immediate risk (decision pressure)

- **immediate_risk_events**: Failures likely within 24h. Categories: `unconfirmed_commitment`, `unpaid_due`, `expected_response`, `promised_followup`, `deposit_missing`. Generated by cron; auto-resolved when the condition disappears.
- **GET /api/installation/today-risk?workspace_id=...**: Returns plain text only: one line per risk category present (e.g. “A scheduled interaction lacks confirmation.”). No counts, timestamps, or numbers.
- **Activation trigger**: Workspace transitions to **active** only when `snapshot_seen_at` is set and `countUnresolvedImmediateRisks(workspaceId) >= 1`. No manual activation; activation feels inevitable after observing reality.
- **continuation_prevented**: Incident category. When an immediate risk category existed during observing and the same pattern does not reoccur for 48h after active, an incident is created: “Operational instability ceased after activation.”
- **GET /api/operator/deal-justification?workspace_id=...**: Internal only (cron/auth). Returns JSON booleans: `instability_detected`, `immediate_risk_present`, `manual_supervision_required`, `economic_events_present`, `network_dependency_present`. Not exposed on public or responsibility surfaces.
- **Propagation ignition**: When workspace is economically_active, has network_entries, and ≥3 acknowledged counterparties, the system emits protocol event `coordination_reliance_established` and sends exactly one outbound message: “Coordination now relies on shared records.” Once per workspace (tracked in `coordination_reliance_message_sent`).
- **Cron /api/cron/immediate-risk**: Runs immediate risk detectors, resolvers, continuation_prevented check, and propagation ignition. Schedule every 15–30 minutes.

## Monetary consequence (financial exposure)

- **financial_exposure_records**: Economic consequence without numbers. Categories: `revenue_at_risk`, `payment_delay`, `customer_loss_risk`, `idle_capacity`. One record per situation per day (dedupe by workspace/category/ref/day). Auto-resolve when situation no longer applies.
- **Detection**: Revenue at risk = opportunity stalled, customer intent present, not replied within 4h working hours. Payment delay = payment overdue ≥12h. Customer loss risk = returning customer silent after business promise. Idle capacity = available booking window passed unfilled while inquiries existed within prior 24h.
- **GET /api/installation/economic-pressure?workspace_id=...**: Plain text statements only (e.g. “A waiting customer may disengage.”). No counts, amounts, currency, or percentages.
- **Escalation memory**: If same category occurs 3 times in 7 days → incident “This situation repeatedly required intervention.” (category `repeated_financial_exposure`).
- **Operator deal-justification** (internal) extended with: `financial_risk_present`, `repeated_instability`, `prevented_instability`.
- **Pre-activation conversion**: If workspace in **observing** and financial_exposure_records exist for 2 consecutive days → incident “Current operating conditions are producing avoidable loss.” (category `avoidable_loss_observed`). Does not activate; record only.
- **Cron /api/cron/financial-exposure**: Runs financial exposure detectors, resolvers, escalation memory, and pre-activation conversion. Schedule every 15–30 minutes.
- **Doctrine**: Never estimate money, show ROI, metrics, savings, or percentages. Only consequences of reality; the system demonstrates loss, not value.

## Snapshot

- **generateInstallationSnapshot(workspaceId)**: Builds 1–3 short sentences from `observed_risk_events` in the last 7 days. No counts, no metrics. Inserts into `installation_snapshots` and sets `snapshot_generated_at`.
- **GET /api/installation/snapshot**: Returns `{ snapshot_text }`. When phase is `activation_ready`, first view sets `snapshot_seen_at`; then `transitionInstallationPhase` runs and, if ≥1 unresolved immediate risk exists, phase → `active`.
- **POST /api/installation/snapshot/seen**: Body `{ workspace_id }`. Sets `snapshot_seen_at`; then `transitionInstallationPhase` runs (idempotent). Phase → `active` only when ≥1 immediate risk.

## Incidents

- **incident_statements**: Human-readable statements only (e.g. “A payment was incomplete — completion restored.”). No counts, no money amounts, no marketing.
- **createIncidentStatement(workspaceId, category, relatedExternalRef?)**: Idempotent per workspace, category, ref, and day.
- **GET /api/incidents/recent**: Returns last 5 incidents; no history, no filters.
- Generated from economic events (payment_recovered, opportunity_revived, commitment_saved, dispute_prevented, no_show_prevented), rare-event detectors (long_gap_followup, high_value_inquiry_delay, completed_work_unpaid, returning_customer_drop, silence_risk_urgent_intent, promise_followthrough_gap, deposit_gap_after_booking), decision pressure (continuation_prevented), and monetary consequence (repeated_financial_exposure, avoidable_loss_observed).

## Previews

- **pending_action_previews**: Before the first automated message of each action type, a preview is stored (e.g. “If no reply occurs, a confirmation message will be sent.”).
- **executed_action_types**: After the first successful send for an action type, the workspace is marked as having executed that type; the preview is removed.
- **GET /api/previews**: Returns `{ previews: [{ action_type, preview_text, will_execute_at }] }`. Visibility only; no approvals or toggles.

## Why This Preserves Doctrine

- **No dashboards or analytics**: Only record surfaces and single-message responses (snapshot, incidents, previews). No charts, feeds, or counters.
- **Statements over metrics**: Value is communicated in short institutional sentences, not numbers.
- **Automatic behavior over user control**: Phase transitions and snapshot generation are cron- and view-driven; no configuration UI.
- **Relief and certainty**: Snapshot and morning certainty message (“Nothing requires attention.” / “1 item requires a decision.”) provide relief; incident statements demonstrate protection without requiring interpretation of logs.

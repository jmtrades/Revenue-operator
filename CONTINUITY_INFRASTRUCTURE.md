# Continuity Infrastructure

**Category:** Continuity Infrastructure — not a CRM, not an AI agent, not an automation builder.

## What it is

Continuity Infrastructure is deployable operational reality that makes promised interactions certain, recovers stalled conversations and payments, and creates cross-party confirmation through shared records. Payment becomes inevitable only after proven value, using consequence-based proof. No ROI numbers; no dashboards; no template-based messaging.

## Three core behaviors

1. **Certainty** — Promised interactions never remain ambiguous. Follow-ups, confirmations, and outcomes are tracked to resolution or escalation.
2. **Recovery** — Stalled conversations and payments are recovered or escalated. The system re-engages or hands off; nothing stays silent.
3. **Shared record** — Agreements and commitments exist as shared records with counterparty acknowledgement. Cross-party confirmation replaces back-and-forth.

## Rules (non-negotiable)

- **No templates.** Message Compiler: structured generation + deterministic rendering. LLM may only return plan fields (intent, stance, entities, constraints); never full message text.
- **No raw AI.** No direct freeform LLM text is sent to customers. All outbound passes through the compiler.
- **Commercial wedge:** Unresolved commitments and delayed payments. Businesses pay to remove uncertainty and silent loss.

## Why businesses pay

- Removal of uncertainty: work reaches an outcome or produces an entry.
- Elimination of silent loss: stalled deals and missed payments are recovered or escalated.
- Shared records reduce clarification and dispute; counterparties confirm in the environment.

## Product surface

- Record surfaces only: Responsibility, Record, Assurance, Context.
- No analytics, charts, performance metrics, activity feeds, optimization suggestions, workflow builders, or pipeline editors.
- Messages: factual, short, past tense. No you/we/click marketing copy.
